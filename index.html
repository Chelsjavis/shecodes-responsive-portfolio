Musician

Chelsea is a freelance flautist and piccoloist, having performed in Australia, Canada and Europe. She holds a Master of Music and a Bachelor of Arts with First-Class Honours from the University of Western Australia. Chelsea is particularly passionate about performing works by historically marginalised composers.

See Chelsea perform

Educator

Chelsea is a passionate educator and has been the Flute Tutor at several WA schools including St Brigid's College and St Stephen's School. Having recently relocated to Sydney, she is currently building up her private studio.
Chelsea also spent nine years working as a Mathematics Tutor for a not-for-profit organisation in Perth that specialises in the tuition of students with learning difficulties.

Developer

Always interested in learning new things, at the completion of her Master of Music degree, Chelsea decided to start exploring software development. She is currently working through the SheCodes Bootcamp course.
